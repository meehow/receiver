## Why Receiver?

Every station in Receiver has been **individually scanned** to verify it's reachable and actually streaming. Duplicates have been removed, broken streams filtered out, and logos fetched in proper resolution â€” so what you see is a clean, reliable station list, not a dump of 50,000 half-dead URLs.

No sign-up. No ads. No popularity rankings deciding what you hear. Just radio.
